## 4. OPD TIMINGS & APPOINTMENT BOOKING

### Walk-in Registration
- **Days:** Monday to Saturday (Sunday closed)
- **Timing:** 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM (Registration — consultations may continue beyond 12 PM)
- **Location:** OPD Registration Counter, Ground Floor
- **Fee:** ₹20 (nominal registration fee)
- **Required Documents:** Valid government photo ID (Aadhaar card), previous medical records if any
- **Note:** Some departments may have different OPD days. Confirm via OPD reception.

### Online Appointment Booking
- **Portal:** https://ors.gov.in (Online Registration System)
- **Required:** Mobile number or ABHA (Ayushman Bharat Health Account) number
- **Process:** Register on ORS → Select Rajasthan → Select AIIMS Jodhpur → Choose Department → Select available date → Confirm
- **Available for:** Most specialty departments

### AIIMS Jodhpur Swasthya App
- **Platform:** Google Play Store
- **Features:** View OPD schedules, track appointment status, access hospital services
- **Login:** Mobile number or UHID (Unique Health ID)

### OPD Schedule
- Specific daily OPD rosters can be viewed at the official OPD Schedule page: https://aiimsjodhpur.edu.in/opd_roster/OPDSchedule/
- For exact cabin numbers, patients should check at the OPD Registration Counter on the Ground Floor

## 6. HOSPITAL FACILITIES

### OPD Block
- 5-floor building housing all specialty departments
- 6 lifts (elevators) available
- Ramp available — wheelchair and disability friendly
- Ground Floor: Registration Counter, Pharmacy

### Diagnostic Block
- Located behind the Emergency block
- Ground Floor and 1st Floor
- Houses Radiology, MRI, CT Scan, and advanced imaging services
- Pathology & Lab Medicine on site

### Blood Bank (Transfusion Medicine)
- **Phone:** 0291-2831436
- Located on campus
- Provides blood components and transfusion services

### PMR Department (Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation)
- Separate ground floor block for patients with disabilities
- Wheelchair accessible (no lift required)
- Psychiatry IPD is on the 1st floor of the PMR block

### Pharmacy
- Located within the hospital premises
- Caters to both inpatients and outpatients

### ICU & Critical Care
- Multiple specialized ICUs: Adult, Pediatric, Trauma
- Dialysis Unit with 11 machines (1st Floor, Emergency Block)

### Other Facilities
- Cafeteria on campus
- ATM facilities
- Help desk at main entrance for patient orientation
- Parking available (follow security guidance upon arrival)

## 8. TELEMEDICINE SERVICES

AIIMS Jodhpur provides telemedicine services through both national platforms and local consultation:

### When to Use Each Service
- **For follow-up consultations with an existing doctor:** Call **14416** or **1800 891 4416**
- **For a new tele-consultation:** Visit **https://esanjeevani.mohfw.gov.in** and select AIIMS Jodhpur
- **For general telemedicine queries:** Contact OPD Reception at **0291-2831001**

### Telemedicine Platforms
- **National Helpline:** 14416

## 9. PATIENT GUIDELINES

### Documents to Bring for OPD Visit
1. Valid government photo ID (Aadhaar card preferred)
2. Previous medical records, prescriptions, investigation reports
3. Referral letter (if from another hospital)

### Appointment Flow
1. Register online via ORS portal OR walk-in at OPD counter
2. Get UHID (Unique Health ID) — valid for life at AIIMS Jodhpur
3. Visit the respective department OPD on scheduled date
4. Consult with doctor
5. Collect prescriptions / proceed to pharmacy or investigations

### Important Notes for Patients
- Arrive early (before 10:00 AM) as walk-in slots fill up quickly
- For exact cabin numbers of doctors, ask at the OPD Registration Counter on the Ground Floor
- Cabin numbers change frequently — do not rely on old information
- For specific room or cabin numbers, contact OPD Registration at **0291-2831001** or visit Room 118, B Block
- Telemedicine numbers are available for follow-up consultations
- For emergency, always call 0291-2831622 or visit Gate 3 directly
